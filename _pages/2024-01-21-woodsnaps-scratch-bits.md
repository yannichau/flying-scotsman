# Stuffed animal travelling agency?! 🍣

A while ago I found out about [Unagi Travel](http://unagi-travel.net/), which offers travelling for stuffed animals, and even passports! A similar tradition has been going on in many American schools too, called [The Flat Stanley Project](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Flat_Stanley_Project), where flat Stanleys are used to facilitate pen-pals.

My WoodSnaps project is inspired by this cool concept. Since I am a fan of the underground, I have decided to bring Woodstock on every station on the tube, which will inject some purpose into my commutes and trainspotting trips. It also represents a great opportunity to rejuvenate my abandoned photography Instagram account, and to learn web visualisation!

## Project Notes

SVG map extracted from [Wikipedia](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/13/London_Underground_Overground_DLR_Crossrail_map.svg). Useful OSI tooltips and blinking lines from original file, visualisation and modal windows built with MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) and the d3.js packages.
