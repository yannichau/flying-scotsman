### Replay

Swiftly, I flutter across the caseless sky  
Flying by the pastures and the whistling songbirds

Thump!  
I plummeted,  
sinking  
scuppering  
spiraling  
into the deepest of the wallows  
as I drifted into cavernous earth

Frail  
Feeble  
Fragile  
I slowly rise from the deepest of the wallows  
Beneath your feet  
Dancing, my heart beats  
And sprouts  
Thickening webs crawling up my spine

I hear the tune of cuddling songbirds tickling me  
children’s clumsy footsteps scrunching by  
Knowing this is the epilogue  
and a prologue
