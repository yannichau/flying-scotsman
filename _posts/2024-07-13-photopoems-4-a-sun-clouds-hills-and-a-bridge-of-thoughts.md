**The Sun**

Like a timid introvert  
Hiding behind the mountains  
Gently peeking through the misty skirts

Awake!

Like drops of smearing paint  
She blushes the entire canvas with tangerine streaks  
Caressing the clouds with light medallion taints

Rise!

Like a brave, glowing flame  
Her vibrancy came  
Popping  
Sizzling  
Crackling  
Frizzling  
Filling the rifts with beaming scorching rays.

Rejoice!

Like an ephemeral thriller  
Her magical glee vasnishes from sight  
As she reveals the charming azure that stays.

![](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/image-17.png)

**Clouds**

Caressing, gently, the  
Lavish, immaculately white snow  
On the  
Utterly almighty Annapurnas, as I gaze upon it,  
Dumbfounded and simply amazed!

![](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/image-3.png)

**Terraced Gardens**

Rows  
alternating  
highlights and  
shadows gleaming  
under the exuberant sun

Jagged  
and uneven  
overloaded with  
the benevolance of  
farmers’ tender footsteps

Steps  
leading to  
the azure blue  
stpes descending  
from what we call heaven

![](/assets/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/image-15.png)

**Bridge of thoughts**

While I cross a cantilever bridge that  
leaps  
across the

deep  
deep

valley  
as I ponder upon my

deep  
deep

thoughts.
